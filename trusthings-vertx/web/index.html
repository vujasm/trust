Trusthings - Trustworthy IoT Services

This is the home page for the prototype of Trustworthy Internet of Things Services Web service. The service does computation of trust index of Internet of Things Services and their ranking.

To do simple tests, with this request, you can execute either of the following links:

  • Compute Service 'A'trust index.
  • Compute Service 'B' trust index.
  • Services 'A', 'B', 'C' rank A and B.
